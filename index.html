What is Ezmotion?

We are a marketing and talent management agency, that provides the best micro influencer platform in India to connect brands with influencers to create great stories.We have exclusive relationship with our influencers allows us to connect ambitious brands to relevant personalities in order to reach the right target audiences.

Whether you are an influencer or you own a brand, we can closely work with you to enhance your online presence and maximize your engagement and reach.

Services

Campaign designing

We help brands by understanding thier business goals, objectives, expected results. Then our Influencer marketing experts design whole campaign and develop product reviews, social media shout outs, launch events, and many other things.

Talent Management

We’ll take care of your PR activities, relations with sponsors, negotiations on endorsement deals, social media engagement, and all other communications. We’ll do your networking so that you can focus on what you do best: creating content and engaging with your fans.

Brand Creation

We conceptualize marketing campaigns for your brand based on current local market trends, keeping your goals in mind. In addition to a tailor-made market entry strategy, we’ll take care of all your PR activities, create a formidable online presence, advertise through our network of influencers, and build a strong reputation for your brand.

Search Influencer

In a world where influencers are the new celebrities, we will match your brand with an influencer who reflects your look, vision, and core values. This allows your brand’s message to be endorsed and communicated more authentically than any other form of advertising.
